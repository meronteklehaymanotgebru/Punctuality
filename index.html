What is Punctuality?

  • Strong will & determination: Completing tasks on time.
  • Positive first impression: Arriving on time builds trust.
  • Right action, right time: Timeliness beyond just arrival.

Lesson learned

"If you are 15 minutes early, you are on time, if you are on time ,you are late and if you are late, you didn't want to be there."

Historical context:In ancient Rome, the use of sundials to track time highlighted the significance of punctuality,setting the stage for our current methods of timekeeping.
This historical context reminds us of the importance of respecting time.
